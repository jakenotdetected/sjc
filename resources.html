Practical Tips

Everyday Wellness Practices

During Exam Season
?Create a realistic study timetable and stick to it
?Take a 10-minute break every 50 minutes of study
?Get 7–8 hours of sleep — your brain consolidates memory overnight
?Eat proper meals and stay hydrated — avoid all-nighters
?Talk to someone if exam worry feels too big to handle alone
?On exam day: breathe deeply, read questions twice, start with what you know
Daily Mental Health Habits
?Spend at least 30 minutes outdoors or doing physical activity each day
?Limit social media use to less than 1 hour per day during term
?Practise gratitude — write down 3 good things each day before bed
?Maintain a consistent bedtime and wake-up time
?Connect with at least one friend or family member each day
?Do one thing you enjoy every day — music, art, sport, or cooking
When You Feel Overwhelmed
?Stop and take 5 slow, deep breaths before doing anything
?Write down what is worrying you — getting it out of your head helps
?Break the problem into the smallest possible next step
?Talk to a trusted friend, teacher, or family member
?Go for a walk — physical movement shifts mental state
?Visit the counselling unit — you don't need to go through it alone
