What Josephians Achieved

Real stories from students and parents who experienced the difference our counselling unit made.

RM
Ravindu Madushan
Past Student · A/L 2024
Career Guidance
★★★★★
"

The career guidance session changed my life. I had no idea what to do after A/Ls, but after career pathway testing I found my passion for engineering. The counsellors helped me prepare my university application and I got accepted into my first choice.

Now studying Engineering at University of Moratuwa
SK
Sachini Kumari
Past Student · O/L 2024
Mental Wellness
★★★★★
"

I was struggling with severe exam anxiety that was affecting my results. After attending the stress management workshop and having two one-to-one sessions, I learned techniques that genuinely worked. I got through my O/Ls with flying colours.

Achieved 8 A grades in O/Level examinations
AP
Ashen Perera
Past Student · Medicine Scholar
Scholarships
★★★★★
"

The CV building workshop and mock interview sessions gave me the confidence I needed to apply for university scholarships. The counsellors reviewed my application multiple times and coached me on how to present myself. It made all the difference.

Full scholarship – studying Medicine at University of Kelaniya
DW
Dilshan Weerasinghe
Current Student · Grade 12
Leadership
★★★★★
"

The leadership camp opened my eyes to skills I never knew I had. I used to be terrified of public speaking. After the communication skills sessions, I represented my school in an inter-school debate and we won. The counsellors genuinely care.

Currently School Prefect & inter-school debate champion
NF
Nuwan Fernando
Past Student · A/L 2023
Personal Counselling
★★★★★
"

When I was going through a really difficult time at home, my grades dropped and I lost motivation. The one-to-one counselling sessions gave me a safe space to talk. The counsellor helped me refocus and build a plan. I'm genuinely grateful.

Passed A/Levels and currently pursuing a business degree
