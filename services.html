What We Offer

Our Counselling &
Guidance Services

Six core service areas designed to support every dimension of a Josephian's growth – from personal challenges to career planning and mental wellness.

All Services

Comprehensive Support for Every Student

All services are free, confidential, and available to every student at St. Joseph's College. No referral is needed – just request an appointment.

Personal Counselling

One-to-one sessions in a safe, confidential space for students dealing with personal, emotional, or social challenges affecting their school life.

What we help with
Anxiety, depression, and emotional difficulties
Family conflicts and relationship issues
Bullying, peer pressure, and social difficulties
Grief, loss, and life transitions
Confidential Book Session
Career Guidance

Structured sessions to help students explore career options, understand their strengths, and plan their path from school to university and beyond.

What we help with
Career aptitude and interest assessments
A/L stream and subject selection advice
University application and scholarship guidance
CV building and interview preparation
All Grades Explore Careers
Academic Support

Helping students overcome academic struggles, develop effective study habits, and improve concentration and performance in examinations.

What we help with
Study skills and time management strategies
Exam preparation and revision planning
Learning difficulties and focus challenges
Goal setting and academic motivation
O/L & A/L Focus Book Session
Mental Wellness Programmes

Group workshops and individual support focused on building emotional resilience, managing stress, and promoting positive mental health.

Programmes include
Stress and anxiety management workshops
Mindfulness and relaxation techniques
Anger management and emotional regulation
Peer support groups
Workshops Available View Resources
Parent Counselling

Sessions for parents seeking guidance on supporting their child through academic pressure, behavioural challenges, and career decisions.

Topics covered
Understanding adolescent behaviour and needs
Supporting children through exams and stress
Communication strategies for parent-child relationships
Career and stream selection guidance
For Parents Book Session
Leadership Development

Programmes designed to build the soft skills, confidence, and leadership qualities that students need beyond the classroom.

Programmes include
Annual Leadership Camp for senior students
Public speaking and debating workshops
Team building and collaboration activities
Community service and volunteering projects

How It Works

Your Journey with Our Unit

Getting support is simple. Here's what to expect from your first contact to your counselling session.

1
Register

Create your free student account on the portal with your admission number, academic details, and interests.

2
Book

Choose your preferred session type, select a convenient date and time slot from the online booking calendar.

3
Session

Meet your counsellor in a private, comfortable setting. All discussions are strictly confidential.

4
Follow-Up

Receive a personalised action plan and schedule follow-up sessions as needed to track your progress.

Our Confidentiality Promise

Everything discussed in counselling sessions remains strictly confidential between you and your counsellor. Information is only shared with a third party in exceptional circumstances where there is a serious risk of harm – and you will always be informed first. We are committed to creating a safe, judgement-free space where every student feels heard and respected.
