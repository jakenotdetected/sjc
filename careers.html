Be Prepared

CV Writing & Interview Tips

Building a Strong CV
1Start with your full name, contact details, and a professional email address
2List education in reverse chronological order — A/Ls, O/Ls, primary school
3Include extracurricular activities, leadership roles, and sports achievements
4Add skills relevant to the role — languages, IT skills, certifications
5Include a brief personal statement highlighting your goals and strengths
6Get two referee contacts from teachers or community leaders
Interview Preparation
1Research the institution or company thoroughly before the interview
2Practise answering "Tell me about yourself" — keep it to 2 minutes
3Prepare examples of achievements using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
4Dress formally and arrive at least 15 minutes early
5Maintain eye contact and listen carefully before answering
6Prepare 2–3 thoughtful questions to ask the interviewer
