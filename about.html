Who We Are

About the Counselling &
Career Guidance Unit

Established to nurture every Josephian's potential – we provide holistic support through professional counselling, career mentorship, and mental wellness programmes.

Our Foundation

Vision, Mission & Core Values

The principles that guide our work and define our commitment to every student we serve.

01
Our Vision

To be the leading career guidance unit in the North Central Province – empowering every Josephian to discover their purpose, develop their potential, and step confidently into the future.

02
Our Mission

To provide accessible, confidential, and compassionate counselling services that support the academic, personal, and career development of students at St. Joseph's College.

03
Our Values
Confidentiality & Trust
Inclusivity & Respect
Student-Centred Care
Professional Excellence
Continuous Growth
Our Approach

The Four Pillars of Our Work

Everything we do is guided by four core areas of student development and support.

01
Personal Counselling

Confidential one-to-one sessions addressing personal challenges, emotional wellbeing, family issues, and social difficulties that impact a student's school life.

02
Career Guidance

Helping students discover their strengths, explore career pathways, understand university requirements, and plan strategically for life after school.

03
Mental Wellness

Workshops, resources, and group sessions focused on stress management, exam anxiety, mindfulness, and building emotional resilience in students.

04
Leadership & Development

Leadership camps, public speaking training, and extracurricular development programmes that equip students with 21st-century soft skills.
